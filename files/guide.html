Basics:

You will advance in the game by answering to the questions represented in each of the hills. At the beginning each hill has only one question unlocked. After you have completed any of the questions, the next one will unlock. You win by answering all questions. Be aware that wrong answers will lower your score.

Controls:

Move around the map by holding down your left mouse button and dragging the mouse.

Open the hill questions by left clicking the white dots on the map.

Answer a question by left clicking the question box. Proceed by giving your answer in the text or multi choice field that opened on top of the questions. Press the arrow button next to that field to submit your answer. Correct answer will close the answer field and play a sound.

Progess saving and loading:

Save your game to your computer by opening the settings menu from the gear icon at the top right corner of your screen.

Continue your game by clicking continue in the game main menu. If the game succeeded to save your progess onto your browser, the game will open up right away. Otherwise you can use the "TMHC_SaveFile.txt" file to continue from where you left off.

(IMPORTANT! You should always save your progress to your computer from the gear icon in top right corner. Not doing so may lead to loss of progress!)
